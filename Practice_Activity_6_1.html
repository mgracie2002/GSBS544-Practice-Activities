  • Are any of thhe models above overfitting the data? If so, which ones and how can you tell?
  • Which of the above models do you think fits the data best and why?

The simple regression model appears to underfit as it makes it seem that the relationship between the two variables is perfectly linear which is not likely or realistic.

The degree-10 model appears to be trying to capture every single nuance in the data, which is not the most efficient for analysis and also does not translate well visually.

The degree-2 and degree-3 models both appear rather similar and seem to do the best job of communicating some of the patterns in the data that make it not perfectly linear, while not trying to capture every single detail.
